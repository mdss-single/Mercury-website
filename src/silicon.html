Silicon Metal

Silicon is the second most abundant element in the Earth’s crust after oxygen

Silicon mostly occurs as oxygen compounds (quartz, silicates, etc.). High-purity silicon is used in semiconductor engineering, and commercial purity silicon (96-99% Si) – in ferrous and nonferrous metal industry for non-ferrous base alloying (silumin, etc.), alloying addition (silicon steels and alloys applied in electrical equipment) and steel and alloy deoxidation (oxygen removal), silicide production, etc.
